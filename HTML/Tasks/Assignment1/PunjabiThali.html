        A typical Punjabi thali includes:

        Rice

        Green Salad (onion rings, cucumber, carrot, lemon)

        1. Sabzi (Paneer Butter Masala â€“ example):->
        Make a base with onion, tomato, ginger, garlic, cashews.
        Cook, blend, and simmer with spices, butter, cream & paneer cubes.

        2. Dal Makhani:->
        Soak black urad dal & rajma overnight.
        Cook in pressure cooker, then simmer for hours with onion, tomato, butter, cream & spices.

        3. Chole:->
        Soak chickpeas overnight.
        Cook with onion, tomato, ginger-garlic paste & Punjabi spices (chole masala, amchur).

        4. Bread:->
        Make dough with wheat flour (for roti) or refined flour (for naan).
        Roll and cook on tawa / oven / tandoor.

        5. Rice:->
        Cook basmati rice plain or with cumin (jeera).
