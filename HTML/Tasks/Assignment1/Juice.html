✨Instruction For Made a Juice
       🥘Basic Ingredients
        Fresh fruits (orange, apple, pineapple, watermelon, mango, etc.)
        Sugar / honey (optional)
        Water / ice cubes
        Lemon juice (optional, for extra freshness)
        Mint leaves (optional, for garnish)

        👩‍🍳 Method 1: With Mixer/Juicer
        Wash & peel the fruits. Remove seeds if needed (e.g., apples, watermelon).
        Cut into small pieces.
        Put fruit pieces in a mixer/juicer.
        Add ½ to 1 cup cold water (or ice cubes).
        Blend until smooth.
        If needed, strain through a sieve (for clear juice).
        Add sugar/honey & a few drops of lemon juice (optional).
        Pour into a glass → garnish with mint → serve chilled.

        👩‍🍳 Method 2: By Hand (for citrus fruits like orange, lemon, sweet lime)
        Cut the fruit into halves.
        Use a hand juicer or squeeze with your palm.
        Strain if seeds fall in.
        Add sugar or honey if needed.
        Serve fresh.
