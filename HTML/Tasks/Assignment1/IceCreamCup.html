✨Instruction For Made a Ice Creame
        🍨 Homemade Ice Cream Cup
        🥘 Ingredients (for 4 cups)
        Full cream milk – 2 cups
        Fresh cream – 1 cup (whipping cream or Amul fresh cream)
        Sugar – ½ cup (adjust to taste)
        Cornflour – 1½ tbsp (for thickness)
        Vanilla essence – 1 tsp (or any flavor: chocolate, mango, strawberry)
        Chopped nuts / choco chips / fruit chunks – optional
        Waffle cups / paper cups – for serving

        👩‍🍳 Steps
        1. Make the Ice Cream Base:->
        In a saucepan, boil milk.
        Take out 3 tbsp milk, mix with cornflour → make slurry.
        Add slurry to boiling milk, stir continuously until slightly thick.
        Add sugar → dissolve completely.
        Let it cool.

        2. Add Cream & Flavor:->
        Whip the cream lightly until soft peaks form (if available).
        Mix cooled milk mixture + cream + vanilla essence.
        Blend in a mixer for 1–2 mins (to make it smooth and airy).

        3. Freeze:->
        Pour into paper cups or small bowls.
        Cover with foil / cling wrap.
        Freeze for 6–8 hours (best overnight).
