✨Instruction For Made a North Indian Food
       🥘Ingredients (for 3–4 servings)
        1. Paneer Butter Masala (or Shahi Paneer)
        Ingredients:
        Paneer cubes, onion, tomato, cashews, ginger-garlic paste, butter, cream, garam masala, chili powder, kasuri methi, coriander leaves.
        Steps:
        Sauté onions, tomatoes, cashews → blend into smooth paste.
        Heat butter, add ginger-garlic paste → cook.
        Add the onion-tomato paste, chili powder, turmeric, coriander powder, garam masala.
        Add cream + milk for richness.
        Add paneer cubes + kasuri methi.
        Garnish with coriander & onion rings.

        2. Tandoori Paneer Tikka
        Ingredients:
        Paneer cubes, onion, capsicum, curd, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, lemon juice, skewers.
        Steps:
        Make marinade with curd + spices + lemon juice.
        Coat paneer, onion, capsicum in marinade → rest 30 min.
        Grill on pan/oven/air fryer until charred.
        Serve with lemon wedges & onion rings.

        3. Chapati / Roti
        Ingredients:
        Whole wheat flour, water, salt, ghee.
        Steps:
        Knead flour + water + salt into soft dough.
        Rest 15–20 min.
        Roll into thin rounds.
        Cook on hot tawa until puffed & golden.

        4. Jeera Rice with Peas
        Ingredients:
        Basmati rice, cumin seeds, peas, ghee, salt.
        Steps:
        Wash & soak rice 20 min.
        Heat ghee, add cumin seeds.
        Add rice + peas + salt + water (2:1 ratio).
        Cook until fluffy & fragrant.
