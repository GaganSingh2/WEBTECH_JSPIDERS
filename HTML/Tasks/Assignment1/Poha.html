✨Instruction For Made a Poha
 
        🥘 Ingredients (for 2 servings)
        Poha (flattened rice) – 1½ cups
        Onion – 1 medium (finely chopped)
        Green chili – 1–2 (chopped)
        Potato – 1 small (optional, diced small)
        Curry leaves – 6–8
        Peanuts – 2 tbsp (optional, for crunch)
        Mustard seeds – ½ tsp
        Turmeric powder – ¼ tsp
        Salt – to taste
        Sugar – ½ tsp (optional, gives balance)
        Oil – 2 tbsp
        Fresh coriander leaves – 2 tbsp (chopped)
        Lemon juice – 1–2 tsp

        👩‍🍳 Preparation Steps--
        1. Prep the Poha:->
        Put poha in a sieve/colander.
        Rinse gently under running water once or twice (don’t soak too much).
        Let it rest for 5 minutes → poha will soften.
        Fluff with a fork and sprinkle salt + sugar over it. Keep aside.

        2. Cook the Base:->
        Heat oil in a pan.
        Add peanuts → fry until golden, take out & keep aside.
        In the same oil, add mustard seeds. Let them crackle.
        Add curry leaves, green chilies, and chopped onions.
        Sauté until onions turn soft & slightly golden.
        (Optional) Add diced potatoes and cook till tender.

        3. Add Poha:->
        Lower the flame, add turmeric powder → stir quickly.
        Add the softened poha and mix gently (don’t mash it).
        Cover and steam for 2 minutes.

        4. Garnish & Serve:->
        Add fried peanuts back.
        Sprinkle coriander leaves & lemon juice.
        Mix lightly..
