✨Instruction For Made a Chaii
       🥘 Ingredients (2 cups)
        Water – 1 cup
        Milk – 1 cup (adjust to taste)
        Tea leaves / tea powder – 2 tsp (Assam or your favorite brand)
        Sugar – 2 tsp (as per taste)
        Optional: grated ginger, cardamom, cloves, cinnamon (for masala chai)

        👩‍🍳 Steps:->
        In a saucepan, boil 1 cup water.
        Add tea leaves (and ginger/cardamom if using).
        Let it boil for 2–3 minutes (for strong tea, boil longer).
        Add 1 cup milk.
        Add sugar as per taste.
        Let it simmer for 2–3 minutes until it gets a nice color and aroma.
        Strain into cups.
        Serve hot with biscuits or snacks.
