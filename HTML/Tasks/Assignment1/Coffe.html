✨Instruction For Made a Coffee
       🥘 Ingredients
        Instant coffee powder – 1 to 2 tsp
        Sugar – 1 to 2 tsp (as per taste)
        Hot milk – 1 cup (or ½ cup milk + ½ cup hot water)

        👩‍🍳 Steps:->
        In a cup, add coffee powder + sugar.
        Add 1 tbsp hot water → stir well (beat until frothy for café-style).
        Pour hot milk slowly while stirring.
        Top with a little froth → enjoy hot!
        👉 For black coffee, just use hot water instead of milk.
