✨Instruction For Made a onion-pakoda-pakora
 
        🥘 Ingredients (for 3–4 servings)
        Onions – 2 large (thinly sliced)
        Gram flour (Besan) – 1 cup
        Rice flour / Corn flour – 2 tbsp (for crispiness)
        Green chilies – 1–2 (finely chopped)
        Ginger – 1 tsp (grated, optional)
        Coriander leaves – 2 tbsp (chopped)
        Carom seeds (Ajwain) – ½ tsp
        Cumin seeds – ½ tsp
        Red chili powder – ½ tsp
        Turmeric powder – ¼ tsp
        Garam masala / Chaat masala – ½ tsp
        Salt – to taste
        Water – as needed (very little)
        Oil – for deep frying

        👩‍🍳 Preparation Steps
        1. Prepare the Onion Mixture:->
        Slice onions thinly and put them in a mixing bowl.
        Add green chilies, ginger, coriander, and spices (ajwain, cumin, turmeric, chili powder, garam masala, salt).
        Mix well with your hands → onions release water.

        2. Make the Batter:->
        Add gram flour (besan) and rice flour to the onion mixture.
        Mix everything — the onions’ moisture will bind the batter.
        If too dry, sprinkle just a little water (don’t make a runny batter; keep it thick and sticky).

        3. Fry the Pakodas:->
        Heat oil in a kadhai (medium flame).
        Drop small portions of the mixture into hot oil (don’t overcrowd).
        Fry until golden brown and crispy.
        Drain on paper towels.
